Exceed BI

Exceed BI is the core product of SmartSCT, an AI startup company in China. With Exceed data, data are processed in a back-end engine running on Spark and Hadoop, and visualization is presented in a front-end application with VizArt.

Wanli Univeristy Big Data Platform

The big data platform for Wanli Unversity uses VizArt and dc.js to visualize a large set of student activity data.

Screenshot shows an analysis of book-borrowing activities at the university’s library. Each circle stands for a category such as literature, science and music, and a larger circle means that there are more borrowing activities under this category, Within each circle are a number of bars, each representing the number of borrowing activities by students from the university’s colleges.
